How to play:

Select the date of the puzzle you'd like to play. There is a new puzzle every day!

Solve the puzzle by filling in all the blue and orange blanks. The last word or letters of each phrase make up the first word or letters of the following phrase. Let's call these "shared" characters keywords. All keywords are shown in either blue or orange. Some phrases have keywords in a single string, so pay attention to the highlighted phrase to delineate which keywords go with which phrases.

Use the arrow keys to help navigate the puzzle, or click on any letter to jump to that phrase. Click on a highlighted keyword letter to toggle the phrase that keyword is used in. Complete each puzzle as fast as possible, and share your time with your friends!
